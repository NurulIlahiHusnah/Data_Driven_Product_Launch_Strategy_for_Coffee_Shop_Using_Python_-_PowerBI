# Data-Driven Product Launch Strategy for Coffee Shop Using Python & PowerBI
### Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop transaction data to determine the best outlet for launching a new beverage product. The objective is to balance growth potential with operational risk by identifying outlets that demonstrate strong product acceptance and operational readiness.

### Business Problem
Small businesses often launch new products across all outlets simultaneously without understanding which locations are most likely to succeed. This approach can increase operational risk and lead to misleading product performance results.

This project explores how transaction data can be used to identify the most suitable outlet for testing a new product before scaling it across the network.

### Analytical Approach

The analysis evaluates outlets across two key dimensions:

1. Operational Readiness
Indicators used:
 - Transaction consistency
 - Average items per transaction
 - Revenue volatility
 - Operational risk signals

2. Product Acceptance
Measured through:
 - Customer penetration
 - Revenue contribution
 - Unit sales performance
 - Product adoption signals

These metrics are combined into a decision framework to identify the best pilot launch outlet.

## 📌 Executive Insight Summary
________________________________________
Key Findings

1️⃣ Outlet 8 demonstrated strong product-market fit

    •	Highest revenue contribution from new product
    •	Highest customer penetration (95%)
    •	Strong unit adoption
    •	Moderate revenue volatility (CV 0.44)
→ Indicates scalable demand with manageable operational risk.

2️⃣ Outlet 3 showed structural instability

    •	Low adoption of new product
    •	Highest revenue volatility (CV 0.64)
    •	Weak leverage impact
→ Launch expansion not recommended.

3️⃣ Outlet 5 showed stable but low leverage impact

    •	Stable revenue
    •	Limited incremental impact from new product
→ Not prioritized for expansion.

________________________________________
## Strategic Recommendation

Launch Strategy Framework:

  •	**Outlet 8 → Pilot Launch** <br>
  •	**Outlet 3 → Hold** <br>
  •	**Outlet 5 → Not Priority** <br>

This strategy balances growth opportunity with operational risk.
